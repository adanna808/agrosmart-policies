What we collect

  • Phone number (optional) — only if you add it to a marketplace listing so buyers can contact you (e.g., via WhatsApp).
  • General location (optional) — free-text you type (e.g., town, market name). No precise GPS.
  • Photos (optional) — product photos for marketplace listings.
  • Crash/diagnostic data (optional) — only if analytics/crash reporting is enabled, to improve stability and performance.

Not collected: precise location (GPS), contacts, financial/banking data, health data, messages, or device identifiers.

Why we collect it

  • App functionality — to create and display your listings and records.
  • Analytics (optional) — to diagnose crashes and improve app performance.

Data is encrypted in transit when sent to our services (HTTPS). We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except service providers acting on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting), bound by confidentiality.

Your choices

  • Providing phone number, location text, and photos is optional. You can use the app without them.
  • You can edit or delete your local records at any time from within the app.

How to request data deletion

You can request deletion of your account-related data (e.g., synced listings or uploads) by contacting us:

  1. Email: lizukandu@gmail.com
  2. Subject: Data Deletion Request — AgroSmart Exchange
  3. Include: your name, the phone number or email used in the app (if any), and a brief description of the data to delete.

Timeline: we aim to confirm within 7 days and complete deletion within 30 days unless we must retain data to comply with law or prevent fraud.

On‑device data: data you keep only on your device can be removed by uninstalling the app or by using in‑app delete controls.

Retention

We keep optional data (e.g., listing phone/location/photos) only as long as needed to provide the feature or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time using the process above.
