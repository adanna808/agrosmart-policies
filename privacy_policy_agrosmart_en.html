Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the AgroSmart Exchange mobile application (“App”) collects, uses, and protects information. The App helps smallholder farmers and grain investors keep storage records, track prices, and optionally create simple marketplace listings. It is designed to work offline and supports multiple languages.

Information We Collect

  • User‑provided data (optional):
    • Phone number — only if you add it to a marketplace listing so buyers can contact you (e.g., via WhatsApp).
    • General location — free‑text you type (e.g., town or market name). No precise GPS.
    • Photos — product photos for marketplace listings.
    • Inventory records — crop type, quantities, storage location notes, dates, and any notes you type.
    • Support communications — messages you send to our support email.
  • Diagnostics (optional): crash logs and basic technical data (e.g., OS version, device model) if analytics/crash reporting is enabled, to improve stability and performance.

Not collected: precise location (GPS), contact lists, financial/banking data, health data, messages, or persistent device identifiers for advertising.

How We Use Information

  • App functionality — to create, display, and manage your inventory records and marketplace listings.
  • Support — to respond to your inquiries and provide assistance.
  • Analytics (optional) — to diagnose crashes, monitor performance, and improve the App.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • Service providers that host or process data on our behalf (e.g., cloud infrastructure, analytics) under confidentiality and security commitments.
  • Legal compliance — where required by law or to protect rights, safety, or prevent fraud.
  • Business transfers — if we undergo a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, we will continue to protect your data and notify you of changes.

Security

Data sent to our services is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we implement reasonable safeguards appropriate to the nature of the data.

Retention

We keep optional data (e.g., listing phone, location text, photos) only as long as needed to provide the App’s features or to comply with law. You can remove on‑device data via in‑app delete controls or by uninstalling the App. You may also request deletion of data stored on our systems (see below).

Your Choices & Rights

  • Providing phone number, location text, and photos is optional.
  • You can edit or delete local records in the App at any time.
  • You can export records (e.g., CSV) where available.
  • You can request deletion of server‑stored data using the process below.

Data Deletion Requests

To request deletion of your data (e.g., synced listings or uploads), contact us:

  1. Email: lizukandu@gmail.com
  2. Subject: Data Deletion Request — AgroSmart Exchange
  3. Include: your name, and the phone number or email used in the App (if any), and a brief description of the data to delete.

We aim to confirm within 7 days and complete deletion within 30 days, unless we must retain certain data for legal or security reasons. For on‑device data only, you can remove it directly in the App or by uninstalling.

See the companion page: Data Practices & Deletion

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you are under the minimum age required by your local law, please do not use the App. If we learn we have collected data from a child without appropriate consent, we will delete it.

International Transfers

Depending on where you are located, your information may be processed on servers outside your country. We take steps to ensure appropriate protections consistent with applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the new version here and update the effective date above.
