Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-26

AgroSmart Exchange (“we”, “our”, “us”) is an offline‑friendly agro‑storage helper. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data we collect

  • User‑provided data: inventory records (crop, quantity, location text, notes), marketplace listing details (phone number for WhatsApp, photos you attach).
  • Device data: basic diagnostics and crash logs only if you enable analytics in settings.
  • No precise GPS: the app does not request GPS/precise location by default.

2. Offline first

Your inventory and listings are stored on your device. If you enable cloud sync later (e.g., Firebase/Supabase), your data will be transmitted securely and stored in your own cloud project.

3. How we use data

  • Provide core features (inventory, price cache, marketplace contact).
  • Improve stability (diagnostics/crash logs if enabled).

4. Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. WhatsApp contact opens your chosen phone number in your WhatsApp app.

5. Security

Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS). Keep your device secure (screen lock, updates). If you enable cloud, use strong credentials.

6. Your choices

  • View, edit, or delete your inventory within the app.
  • Request account/data deletion by emailing support@elizabethnexclusives.com.
  • Disable analytics at any time.

7. Children

Not directed to children under 13.
